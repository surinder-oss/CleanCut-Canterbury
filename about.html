Our Story

Built on care, trust & a greener way to clean

We started Clean Cut Canterbury with a simple idea: bring lawn, garden and cleaning services together under one trusted local team, so you only need one phone call to keep your whole property in great shape.

From the very beginning we committed to plant-based, eco-friendly products. We believe a clean home shouldn’t come at the cost of your family’s health or the Canterbury environment — so we use products that are safe, non-toxic and biodegradable, with no harmful side effects.

Whether it’s a freshly mowed lawn, sparkling windows, revived carpets or a spotless post-build clean, we treat every property like our own.

Plant-Based & Safe

We clean green — with plant-based products

At Clean Cut Canterbury we use plant-based, eco-friendly cleaning products on every job. They cut through dirt and grime just as well as harsh chemicals — without the nasty side effects.

That means a home and garden that is safe for your children, pets and the people you love, and kinder to the soil, waterways and wildlife of Canterbury.

  • Safe for your family & pets — no harsh fumes or toxic residue.
  • Kind to the environment — biodegradable, protects our waterways.
  • No harmful side effects — gentle on surfaces, tough on dirt.
  • Same brilliant results — a spotless finish you can feel good about.
